# Support

Need help with an F5 DevCentral project? Here's how to find it.

## Before Asking

A few places worth checking first. Most questions are answered faster this way:

- The project's **README** for installation, quick start, and basic usage
- The project's **documentation** (if linked from the README)
- **Existing issues:** your question may already be answered: `https://github.com/f5devcentral/{repository}/issues`

## Where to Ask

### GitHub Issues

For project-specific help (bug reports, feature requests, and "how do I..." questions tied to a specific repository), open an issue on that project's GitHub page using the appropriate template.

**Issues are good for:**

- Bug reports (use the bug report template)
- Feature requests (use the feature request template)
- Project-specific questions (use the `question` label)
- Security vulnerabilities. Actually, **don't** open an issue for these. See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.

When asking for help, include:

- What you're trying to do
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Your environment (OS, versions, configuration)
- Minimal reproducible example if applicable
- Steps you've already tried

The more context you give, the faster you get useful answers.

### F5 DevCentral Community

For broader questions that aren't tied to a specific repo (best practices, integration patterns, how F5 products work together, or just connecting with other folks doing similar things), head to [community.f5.com](https://community.f5.com/).

The DevCentral community has been around since 2003 and brings together F5 users, partners, and engineers from all over the world. If your question is "how do other people approach X with F5?" rather than "is X broken in this repo?", community.f5.com is the better starting point.

## Response Times

F5 DevCentral projects are maintained by a mix of F5 employees and community contributors, many doing this alongside other work. We'll do our best to respond promptly:

- **Critical security issues:** as fast as possible; see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
- **Bug reports:** as soon as maintainers can triage
- **Feature requests:** as time and project priorities permit
- **Questions:** community-driven, no guaranteed response time

If something is genuinely urgent and you're an F5 customer, your existing support channels are the fastest path.

## Contributing Back

Found a solution to your problem? Pay it forward:

- Answer questions in issues or on community.f5.com
- Improve the documentation (submit a PR; see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md))
- Fix the bug you discovered

## Code of Conduct

All support interactions are governed by our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Be patient, be respectful, be constructive. We're all here to learn and build.

---

*F5 DevCentral projects are community resources, not commercial products. If you're an F5 customer looking for commercial or enterprise support on F5 solutions themselves, see [MyF5](https://my.f5.com/manage/s/) and your existing support channels.*
